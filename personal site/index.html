I had the grand idea of creating games, like so many gamers. So, to help me with that i got a BS in Computer Science. But, my shyness, anxiety and limited life skills did not help me succeed. I could not get myself into a programing job at that time. So, instead i played around with programing on my own. I dabbled in html, css and javascript creating a simple little online game.
